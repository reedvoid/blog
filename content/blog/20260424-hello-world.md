Resurrecting my personal website! 

I made my first website in 1994, and always had a personal website till after college when it just didn't seem that interesting or important anymore. After a while even making an extremely simple website took a ton of tinkering to get it to look & feel the way I wanted it to, and it just didn't seem fun anymore. 

Now with AI, coding is suddenly fun again. I've been working on a lot of projects, and thought it'd be cool to resurrect the personal website I've had since I was a kid. As I write this very post, I'm feeling just as excited as I did in 1994 when I discovered Mosaic and HTML, what a time to be alive!
